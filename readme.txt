Check what your users are currently doing on your site, stay up to date with their actions and get information in real time.

== Description ==

= Discover tool that will let know more about your business than ever before =
We prepared a set of tools, which gives you an opportunity to check what is happening on your site. From now on, you'll know exactly when your users navigate to the payment section or why they cancelled their order. Exclusively for WordPress platform users, we prepared a revolutionary simple integrating process. In just couple of minutes you'll be ready to work with our tool. And it's all free ! 

= Get to know what your users are doing in your online shop =               
Thanks to the Events feature you’ll see what is going on your website in real time . Get a  complete insight into the occurring events and users’ actions in the site.

**Example:**

* 5 minutes ago the user arthur@me.com added a Product to his cart
* 2 minutes ago the user arthur@me.com continued to payment options
* 1 minute ago the user arthur@me.com paid

= Find out how your users behave =
The Funnels will show you how users act on your website. You need to define (at least) 2 conditions, and then you can see what percentage of users acts in accordance with them.

= Find precise informations regarding your business =
Tidio Live will give you a complete access to the crucial data.

== Installation ==

1. Go to WordPress Control Panel
1. Click "Plugins", then "Add New"
1. Type "Tidio Live" and click "Search Plugins"
1. Download and install the Plugin
1. Click the "Activate Plugin" link
1. Move to the "Tidio Live" section in the menu
1. Done! Now you can start collect your data
